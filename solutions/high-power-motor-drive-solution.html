Driver Solution for High-Power Industrial Motors and Servo Systems

Boosting performance and protecting expensive IGBTs in demanding industrial automation and process control applications.

The Challenge: Performance and Protection in Industrial Drives

High-power industrial motor drives and servo systems require precise control and must be able to withstand harsh operating conditions, including frequent overloads and potential short circuits. The gate driver is a critical component that not only has to deliver clean switching signals for good motor performance but must also react instantaneously to protect the IGBT power stage from destructive fault events.

Solution Overview: Intelligent and Protective Driving

This solution is built around an intelligent Firstack plug-and-play driver like the Firstack 2FHD0320C. It is designed for standard high-power module footprints (e.g., PrimePACK™) common in the drives industry. The key to this solution is the driver's integrated advanced protection features, which go beyond simple driving to actively manage the health of the power module.

Core Advantages of This Solution

  • Enhanced Module Lifetime: The Two-Level Turn-off capability gently switches off the IGBT during a short circuit, drastically reducing the thermomechanical stress and preventing module rupture, which is a common failure mode.
  • Overvoltage Suppression: Advanced Active Clamping actively limits the DC-link overvoltage during turn-off, allowing designers to use IGBTs closer to their voltage limits or reduce the need for bulky snubber circuits.
  • Precise Motor Control: A low-jitter, clean gate signal ensures consistent switching, which translates to smoother torque delivery and better overall motor performance.
  • Fast Time-to-Market: The plug-and-play design eliminates the need for custom driver development, allowing drive manufacturers to focus on their control algorithms and system software.
