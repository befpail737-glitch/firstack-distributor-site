Firstack 2FHD0620

A dual-channel plug-and-play gate driver optimized for up to 1700V SiC MOSFET modules in EconoDUAL™ packages. It is engineered for high-frequency, high-efficiency applications and supports the parallel operation of up to four modules.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does this driver support paralleling of different module part numbers?

A: No. For reliable operation and proper current sharing, it is essential to use identical power modules (same part number and batch if possible) when paralleling.

Q: What is the purpose of the +20V/-5V gate voltage?

A: This asymmetric gate voltage is optimal for SiC MOSFETs. The +20V ensures the device is fully turned on with minimum Rds(on), while the negative -5V provides a strong buffer against parasitic turn-on induced by high dv/dt, enhancing system reliability.

Q: Is the fiber optic receiver included?

A: The driver has the onboard receiver. The fiber optic cables and transmitter on the controller side are typically sourced separately. We can recommend compatible components.
