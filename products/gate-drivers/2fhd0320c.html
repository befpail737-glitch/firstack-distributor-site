Q: What is "Two-Level Turn-off"?

A: It's a protection mechanism where, upon detecting a short-circuit, the driver first reduces the gate voltage to a safe intermediate level before fully turning it off. This slows down the di/dt, reducing dangerous overvoltages and protecting the IGBT from destruction.

Q: Can I use this driver with a 650V IGBT module?

A: While electrically possible, this driver is optimized for the higher voltage classes (1200V/1700V) and specific package. Using it for lower voltage modules may not be cost-effective and the protection thresholds might need adjustment. Contact our FAEs for a recommendation on a more suitable driver.
